üîß Troubleshooting

Extension not showing in menu

Ensure the Ecosystem extension is installed first, then run pnpm updator

License activation fails

Check your internet connection and verify your CodeCanyon credentials are correct

No markets available to select

Create ecosystem markets first in the Ecosystem extension before adding them to AI Market Maker

Bots not trading

Verify the cron jobs are running and both the market and bots are set to ACTIVE status
