üìù Configuration Parameters

‚è±Ô∏è Trading Intervals

Trading Interval
Integer (Minutes)

How often the trading engine runs to process markets and execute trades. Default: 1 minute.

Recommended: 1-5 minutes for active markets

Min Trade Interval
Integer (Seconds)

Minimum time between trades by the same bot. Prevents rapid-fire trading.

Recommended: 10-30 seconds

üìä Global Limits

Max Daily Trades
Integer

Maximum total trades per day across all markets. Resets at midnight UTC.

Default: 10,000 trades/day

Max Concurrent Markets
Integer

Maximum number of markets that can be active simultaneously.

Default: 20 markets

Max Bots Per Market
Integer

Maximum number of bots allowed per market.

Default: 10 bots/market

Daily Trade Count
Integer (Auto)

Current day's trade count. Auto-incremented and resets daily.

üõ°Ô∏è Risk Management

Default Volatility Threshold
Decimal (%)

Default volatility limit for new markets. Trading pauses when exceeded.

Default: 5% - adjust based on market type

Default Max Daily Loss
Decimal (%)

Default maximum loss limit for new markets. Stops trading when hit.

Default: 2% - conservative protection

Circuit Breaker Threshold
Decimal (%)

Global price movement threshold that triggers emergency stop.

Warning: All trading stops when triggered

Circuit Breaker Cooldown
Integer (Minutes)

How long to wait before allowing trading to resume after circuit breaker trips.

Default: 30 minutes

üí∞ Price Settings

Default Spread
Decimal (%)

Default bid-ask spread for new markets.

Default: 0.3% - typical for liquid markets

Price Precision
Integer

Number of decimal places for price calculations.

Default: 8 decimal places

Amount Precision
Integer

Number of decimal places for amount calculations.

Default: 8 decimal places

Slippage Tolerance
Decimal (%)

Maximum acceptable price slippage for trade execution.

Default: 0.5%

üîß System Settings

Enable Trading
Boolean

Global switch to enable/disable all AI trading activity.

Emergency Stop: Toggle off to halt all trading immediately

Debug Mode
Boolean

Enable verbose logging for troubleshooting. Disable in production.

History Retention Days
Integer

How long to keep trade history records before auto-cleanup.

Default: 90 days

Last Reset At
DateTime (Auto)

Timestamp of the last daily counter reset.

‚è∞ Cron Jobs

The AI Market Maker system relies on scheduled cron jobs to execute trades automatically:

Job Schedule Description
aiTradingEngine Every minute Main trading engine - processes markets and executes trades
aiTradingReset Daily at 00:00 UTC Resets daily counters and clears circuit breakers
aiTradingCleanup Weekly Cleans up old history records based on retention setting

üí° Note: Ensure your server's cron system is properly configured to run the backend scheduler. The trading engine won't execute without the cron job running.
